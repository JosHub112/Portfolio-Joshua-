Platformer Game

A dynamic platformer game featuring challenging level design and smooth character mechanics. This was a small school project that I built following a tutorial. The project consists of one simple unfinished level that was more focused on perfecting the mechanics rather than building out a full gameplay loop.

The core mechanics include shooting, jumping, double jumping, getting killed, and moving around. I extensively used DOtween for the second time, implementing it throughout the game to enhance polish and responsiveness.

I'm passionate about game feel, and this project was an excellent opportunity to showcase that passion. Every animation, transition, and interaction was carefully crafted to create a satisfying and fluid gameplay experience. Unlike my other projects, this one didn't have Trello management tracking.
